# Case Study: SCG Chemicals Digital Reliability Platform

## The Pain

Risk of equipment failure shutting down an integrated production chain across multiple interconnected process units. Reactive maintenance dominated. Hidden inefficiency across silos.

## The Solution

AVEVA Digital Reliability Platform deployment across 15 plants and joint ventures. PI System for time-series data. Cloud analytics on Microsoft Azure. AI-driven predictive maintenance integrated with existing maintenance management.

## The Outcome

| Metric | Result |
|---|---|
| Unplanned plant shutdowns | Zero (within 12 months of full deployment) |
| Maintenance cost reduction | 35% |
| Equipment availability improvement | 42% |
| Annual avoided downtime savings | $12M |
| Plants scaled | 15 (full SCG Chemicals + JV footprint) |
