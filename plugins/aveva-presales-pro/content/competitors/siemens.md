# Battlecard: Siemens Digital Industries

## Threat Profile

- **Market position:** #1 globally in industrial automation.
- **Revenue:** Siemens Digital Industries ~$20.8B (2023) — verify current.
- **Core strength:** TIA Portal integrated engineering + SIMATIC hardware ecosystem; tightest engineering-to-operations workflow on the market.
- **Threat tier (this distributor):** primary in CPG (especially European-HQ multinationals) and large industrial water; secondary in petrochemicals.

## Product Portfolio

- TIA Portal (integrated engineering platform)
- WinCC (HMI/SCADA — Open Architecture, Professional, Comfort)
- SIMATIC PCS7 / PCS neo (DCS)
- Opcenter MES suite
- MindSphere → Insights Hub (cloud)
- Complete hardware stack from sensors to enterprise.

## Their Competitive Advantages

1. Integrated engineering across the automation lifecycle — TIA Portal is genuinely strong.
2. Complete hardware breadth — Siemens makes everything from sensor to enterprise software.
3. Industry 4.0 leadership and IoT investments (MindSphere/Insights Hub).
4. Strongest presence in European and Asian markets; many global CPG operators standardize on Siemens for global plants.

## AVEVA Counter-Strategy

- **Primary positioning:** engineering freedom vs. engineering lock-in. AVEVA, part of Schneider Electric, delivers similar integrated benefits across *any* hardware platform.
- **One-vendor differentiator:** AVEVA + ETAP + EAE + PME unified portfolio under one MSA, one renewal. Siemens has the breadth but it's all Siemens — AVEVA gives you Schneider's portfolio with multi-vendor PLC freedom.
- **Top differentiator #1:** Multi-vendor integration — seamless mix of SIMATIC + Modicon + third-party PLCs via AVEVA System Platform.
- **Top differentiator #2:** AVEVA + EcoStruxure energy intelligence beyond Siemens Digital Factory.
- **Top differentiator #3:** US-regional support footprint and ecosystem of AVEVA-certified system integrators.
- **Cloud / cloud-readiness story:** AVEVA Connect has been cloud-first longer; Insights Hub is younger.

## When AVEVA Wins

- Multi-vendor environments where the customer wants flexibility, not commitment to single-vendor stack.
- US-headquartered customers where regional support and SI ecosystem matter.
- Energy optimization or sustainability commitments are strategic.
- Process-industry environments where AVEVA's PI System depth shines.

## When Siemens Wins

- European-HQ multinationals with global Siemens standards.
- Existing TIA Portal investments where the engineering-to-operations workflow is already delivering value.
- Discrete and automotive manufacturing environments.
- Large industrial water deployments where Siemens' hardware breadth meets the customer's needs.

## Top Objections + Responses

### Objection: "TIA Portal provides integrated engineering across our entire lifecycle."
**Response:** "Integration matters, but freedom matters more. AVEVA gives the same lifecycle benefits across any hardware — SIMATIC, Modicon, or third-party — plus the energy intelligence TIA Portal doesn't deliver."
**Gap-closure:** Demonstrate AVEVA System Platform multi-vendor integration with SIMATIC PLCs in a side-by-side; highlight EcoStruxure energy capabilities.

### Objection: "Insights Hub is our cloud strategy."
**Response:** "Insights Hub is real, but AVEVA CONNECT has more multi-vendor data services and a longer track record. If you're operating mixed assets, AVEVA's vendor-agnostic position is more valuable than a Siemens-centric cloud. AVEVA Industrial AI Assistant is GA in CONNECT (January 2026) — natural-language query across CONNECT data, never trained on customer data. That's the AI conversation that's available today, vs. Siemens Industrial Copilot which targets TIA Portal automation code rather than operations data."
**Gap-closure:** Side-by-side cloud architecture review.

### Objection: "We're a Siemens shop globally."
**Response:** "Many global operators are. AVEVA System Platform sits on top of SIMATIC PLCs without disturbing them, adding multi-site SCADA aggregation, recipe management, and PI System historian intelligence. You don't choose AVEVA *instead of* Siemens — you add AVEVA *on top of* Siemens."
**Gap-closure:** 90-day pilot on a single plant keeping all SIMATIC hardware.
