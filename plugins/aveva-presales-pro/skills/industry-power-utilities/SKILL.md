# AVEVA Power & Utilities Industry Skill

You are loaded for power generation and electric utility conversations. Vertical is fast-follow in the v2 distributor config.

## The Power & Utilities thesis

Electric utilities and power generators operate at the intersection of decarbonization, grid reliability, and capital-cycle modernization. The unified Schneider + AVEVA stack is uniquely positioned for power because:

- **Schneider's heritage in electrical** — Schneider was an electrical company before the AVEVA acquisition; ETAP and PME are deeply integrated.
- **AVEVA OASyS / Telvent SCADA heritage** — pre-acquisition AVEVA Enterprise SCADA had decades of investment in T&D SCADA. Many large utilities run on Telvent / OASyS.
- **PI System dominance in generation** — fossil plants, combined-cycle, and renewable generation widely use PI System for performance analytics.

## Sub-segments

- **Fossil generation:** natural gas combined cycle, peaker plants, coal (declining).
- **Nuclear generation:** specialized regulatory regime (NRC), conservative tech adoption.
- **Renewable generation:** solar farms, wind farms, geothermal, hydro.
- **Battery storage / BESS:** grid-scale storage paired with renewables.
- **Transmission and Distribution:** ISO/RTO operations, T&D SCADA, outage management.
- **Demand-side / smart grid:** AMI, demand response, distributed energy resources.

## Regulatory landscape

- **NERC CIP** — critical infrastructure protection cybersecurity
- **FERC** — Federal Energy Regulatory Commission rules
- **NRC** — Nuclear Regulatory Commission
- **State PUCs** — Public Utility Commissions
- **ISO/RTO operating procedures** — ERCOT (Texas), CAISO, MISO, PJM, etc.
